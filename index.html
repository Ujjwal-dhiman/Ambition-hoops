About AHBA

Learn Here,Compete Anywhere

  • OUR MISSION
    • Ambition hoops want to bring in the right kind of approach to train every student in the sport and physical education.
  • OUR VISION
    • Ambition hoops believes in building a sports ecosystem by providing the right platform and learning.
  • WHY US
    • Our program are of effective standards, based on the foster understanding of physical fitness, skill development, competition, and cooperation.

    Program Methodology

    SESSIONS

    Positive and structured sessions in order to nurture young talent.

    DEVELOPMENT

    Development-centric methodology & certified curriculum.

    EVALUATION

    Evaluations & feedbacks at regular intervals.

    MATCHES

    Match Days – Practice matches, inter-academy matches at regular basis.

    PARTICIPATION

    Participation in Local tournaments.

    GRASS-ROOT DEVELOPMENT

    Total focus on grass-root development with progressive coaching for each age category.
